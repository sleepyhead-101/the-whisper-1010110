Whisper Wall Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 31, 2025

By using Whisper Wall, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our platform, we may collect:

  • Account Data: Your email, username, and password when you sign up.
  • Messages: Any anonymous messages you submit.
  • Usage Details: Device type, IP address, browser type, and actions on the platform.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to keep you signed in and analyze user behavior (see Section 5).

Note: Your password is encrypted and not visible to anyone, including us.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data to:

  • Run and improve Whisper Wall’s features
  • Analyze usage trends and activity
  • Communicate about account-related issues
  • Prevent abuse or unauthorized use

3. Legal Sharing

We may share your data with:

  • Third-Party Services: Like hosting, analytics, or moderation tools.
  • Authorities: If required by law or in cases of abuse or threats.

4. Data Retention

We retain:

  • Account info until your account is deleted
  • Messages for 30 days or until they are viewed
  • Analytics data indefinitely in anonymous form

5. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience. You can disable them in your browser, but the app may not function correctly.

6. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access or delete your data
  • Withdraw consent or limit usage
  • Request a copy of the information we store

To request access, email us at privacy@whisperwall.com

7. Children

Whisper Wall is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. International Use

If you’re accessing from another country, your data may be transferred and processed abroad under secure practices.

9. Security

  • Passwords are hashed using industry best practices
  • Data in transit is encrypted (SSL)
  • We monitor and test for vulnerabilities regularly

While we use strong protection, no system is 100% secure.

10. Policy Changes

If we change anything important, we will let you know through the platform or via email.
